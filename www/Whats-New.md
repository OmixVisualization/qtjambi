# What's new in QtJambi 6.4.4 / 6.2.12 / 5.15.14

## Bugfixes

Solved issues:
* [Issue 129](../../../issues/129): UIC to Kotlin errors and exceptions

QtJambi 6.4.4, 6.2.12 and 5.15.14 are source- and binary-compatible to its predecessor releases.
